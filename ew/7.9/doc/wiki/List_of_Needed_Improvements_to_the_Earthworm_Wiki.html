BECOME A CONTRIBUTOR!

Earthworm depends on a broad user community for continued development, documentation and support.

Need a login? Email Paul Friberg at p dot friberg at isti dot com

Got some free time? Looking to make a meaningful contribution? Here are some areas that need improvement:

  • Documentation for most modules found at Earthworm is incomplete. Pick your favorite module and go to town!
  • Add content to: How-To Pages or Q&A.
  • THE BIG PROJECT: There is a lot of good content at ​Earthworm Google Group that could be condensed into the How-To Pages & Q&A.
