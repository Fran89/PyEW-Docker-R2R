WELCOME TO THE EARTHWORM TRAC SITE!

The wiki is hosted by ​ISTI.

In May, 2012 ​OSOP finished converting all of the Earthworm documentation from ​http://folkworm.ceri.memphis.edu/ew-doc/ into trac form.

Our efforts were motivated by the hope that community members will take ownership of the documentation (Want to pitch in? See: List of Needed Improvements to the Earthworm Wiki).

Enjoy,

Branden, OSOP

Earthworm User Community Wiki

Earthworm is an open architecture, open source public software for data acquisition, processing, archival and distribution. Originally developed by the United States Geological Survey, Earthworm binaries and source files are freely available to everyone. Development and maintenance are now carried out by the Earthworm User Community, ​ISTI (Instrumental Software Technologies, Inc., New York, USA) and ​CERI (Center for Earthquake Research and Information, University of Memphis, USA).

Want to pitch in? Sign up!

Questions? Issues? Subscribe to the ​Earthworm Google Group list.
