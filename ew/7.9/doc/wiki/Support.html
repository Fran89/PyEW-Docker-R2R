​ISTI Comprehensive Earthworm Support and Solutions

ISTI has been developing with Earthworm since our inception in 1998 and have been providing support for the maintenance and distribution of Earthworm for the USGS since 2005. We conduct training, install software, and develop new modules at competitive prices. Please contact Paul or Stefan with any questions.

​OSOP: Innovative Geophysical Solutions

Geophysical Solutions to Real World Problems

OSOP develops, installs and supports instrumentation and software for early warning civil defense systems to mitigate the effects of natural disasters. OSOP's vertically integrated solutions and turn-key data acquisition and processing systems are being installed world-wide. Visit us at ​http://www.osop.com.pa for a complete list of our ​hardware and ​software solutions.

We have installed, supported and developed for Earthworm for over a decade.
