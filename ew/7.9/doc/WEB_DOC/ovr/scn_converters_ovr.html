Earthworm Program:
  scn2scnl,scnl2scn Overview

(last revised 1 June 2006)
A pair of conversion modules (code resides in data_exchange/scn_convert) to change trace data, picks and codas from the original Earthworm formats with site,component,network codes to the new Earthworm formats using site,component,network,location codes and visa versa. 


Written by Will Kohler, modified by Pete Lombard to do SCN->SCNL lookups.
