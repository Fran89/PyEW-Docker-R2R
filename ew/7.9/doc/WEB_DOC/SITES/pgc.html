Network Characteristics

Information listed below includes both the core national network and additional western stations

  • ~30 broadband stations across the country, sampled at 40s/s (~90 channels)
  • ~35 short-period stations in western Canada, sampled at 100s/s (~35 channels)
  • All stations employ continuous, packetised digital telemetry with GPS timing at the outstation
  • broadband stations use CMG-3ESP, CMG-3T or STS-1VBB seismometers
  • short-period stations use S-13 seismometers
  • 24+ bit digitizers for both broadband and short-period stations (developed in-house)
